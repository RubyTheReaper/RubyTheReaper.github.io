All about pandas

Physiology

Pandas, also called gian pandas, are big bears with a white body,black arms, legs and ears with black blotches around their eyes. They measure 1.2 to 1.9 (3 feet 11 inches to 6 feet 3 inches) meters in length and can weigh up to 160 kg (350 lbs)

Biology

Scientifically called Ailuropoda melanoleuca, pandas are endemic to China.They are foliovores since bamboo shoots and leaves make up almost all of their diet. Pandas are true bears withib the family Ursidae
